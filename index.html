Efforts and Time Saving

Incorporating RFID tag and GPS tracking will lead to increased efficiency and security, and if you just want the software, the minimal UI will save a lot of efforts on the management end.

ACCESS ANYTIME ANYWHERE

Dedicated storage and cloud space for each school to make sure that any data can be accessed from anywhere in this world, because who doesn’t want it?

Easy Reporting and Tracking

With features like direct notifications, group notifications, expressive student dashboard, the information will reach the right recipients and optimize the tracking process.

Ease of Technology

We provide an amalgam of new age hardware and software technologies like RFID, GPS tracking to provide a full stack solution for a school’s every need.


Seamless Collaboration

We intend to keep the parents and the teachers on the same page regarding the student’s progress, thus enhancing Parent Teacher Chemistry.

Information at Fingertips

The unique website and application powered with minimal design for each school will provide information at the parent’s fingertips.
